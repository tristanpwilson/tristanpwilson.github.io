COVID-19 data tracking and visualization hub for the state of Maryland (HTML, CSS, JS)

From 2020-2021, I operated and maintained a COVID-19 data visualization site focused on the state of Maryland. Early on in the pandemic, I noticed that while the state published their COVID-19 data online, they did so in a way that could be confusing and/or difficult to parse for the layperson. I decided to scrape Maryland's public COVID-19 data and display it in a way that was more clear, visually interesting, and (hopefully) easy to understand. I used leaflet.js for the map visualizations, and chart.js as the base for all of my graphs and charts.

Design and development of modeling portfolio (HTML, CSS, JS, Instagram API)

In 2020, I designed, developed, and hosted a modeling portfolio for a friend who wanted improve her web presence, as well as have a centralized location for her work portfolio, contact information, and more. I built a clean, "minimalist" site that dynamically pulled her latest instagram photos using the instagram API; this way, she could update the content on her site by simply making a new post, without having to deal with code and/or go through me.

Design and development of UserWorks Inc's new corporate website (HTML, CSS, JS, PHP)

In 2017, I had the opportunity to redesign UserWorks' corporate website from the ground up. The previous site was outdated, not responsive, and had been built more than 5 years earlier using a commercial template. I designed and developed an entirely new website and style guide for the company.
